Reading Group Papers

The following link leads to a web application that the Friday Night Computer Science Reading Group (the Cabal) has used to choose the papers it wants to read. I (Jim Rootham) wrote the software and created the site.

The following voting rules were agreed upon:

  • Each member is entitled to 15 votes.
  • Each member may vote a maximum of 5 times for a specific paper.
  • The paper receiving the most votes is the winner.
